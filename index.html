500P has coverage and its scanners stay active at night. 300P doesn't and two of three go dark. Same health system, same patient population, different outcomes. Between 11pm and 7am, 500P scanners are active 31% of the time. 300P scanners: 3%. Adding remote coverage to 300P through Tether could unlock overnight capacity that's already installed and paid for.
